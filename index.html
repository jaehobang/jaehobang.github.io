About Me

Hello! I'm Jaeho Bang, an AI Compiler Engineer by profession and a Researcher at heart. My passion for Artificial Intelligence has shaped both my career and my curiosity.

Beyond AI, I have a deep interest in investing and enjoy exploring new investment strategies. I believe that learning about finance and markets is a lifelong journey, much like technology itself.

The purpose of this site is twofold:

  1. To share my story, background, and professional experiences.
  2. To document and share my insights and discoveries in AI and investing with others.
